5. The umask value of 022 explains why files defaulted to 644 and directories to 755 before any manual changes were made.
6. Together, these restrictions follow the principle of least privilege: only trusted accounts (owner and group) can read, write, or execute project files, reducing the risk of accidental or malicious data exposure.
